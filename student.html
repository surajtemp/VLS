Students

InventtED helps to build customised individual student dashboards that consolidate the student’s data in one on-line site and presents it in consistent and accessible visual formats. The dashboard is maintained over the term of the student’s enrolment to provide a comprehensive record as well as to inform future actions.

Access Anytime, Anywhere

Grade from any location and any device. Our responsive design allows access from any device with an internet connection, including Windows, Mac, IOS, Android, and Chrome books.

Easy-to-Use Tools to Grade Faster

All information is right at the teacher’s fingertips with InventtED intuitive and user-friendly interface and backed by the reliability of 24/7/365 support for maximum online grade book uptime.

Simplify Progress Reports

Track your students’ understanding of standards and skills with online visual reports.

Keep Families Informed
