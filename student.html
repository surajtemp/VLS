Learning Library

A learning library is a repository of content which student’s need in their day-to-day tasks, assignments, and exams to perform successfully. With the evolution of online learning libraries, it is easier than ever for students to access content wherever and whenever they need it. Online learning libraries can be completely internally created and curated, completely accessed by learners across the school.

Query Management System:

The Query Management module is designed to handle student’s queries and disputes, and complaints. By automatically notifying the respective teachers regarding the query, as well as involving your entire school.

Community Forums:

This unique feature VLS allows schools to Create a stand-alone community for respective classes or sections or depending upon the passion/interests of the students which enables teachers and students to connect and discuss on any specified activity/task. Students and teachers can follow, ask questions, start discussions, upvote, comment and share content.
